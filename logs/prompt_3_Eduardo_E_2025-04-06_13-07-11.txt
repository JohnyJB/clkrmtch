Amanvari Resort

Amanvari Residences

Golf Club
Robert Trent Jones II designed a world-class 18-hole golf course that weaves from the hillside to the marina and the beach while revealing breathtaking views of the mountains and ocean. Featuring generous landing areas off the tees, the course is a delight to play, regardless of experience or skill level.





 

Golf Practice facility
Robert Trent Jones II designed a course that is rewarding to play regardless of your handicap; practicing is fun, not forced. You can hit a bucket of balls at our full practice range or work on your short game. More than a practice putting green, its a natural miniature course thats welcoming to pros and young players alike.
